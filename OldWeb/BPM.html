BPM

BPM(Beats Per Minute) was a small project I made as a gift idea that I could sell to other people before Valentine's Day, 2013.

I made it using an Arduino, a Light Dependent Resistor and some LEDs. It looked like this:



When someone put their hand on the Light Dependent Resistor, the LEDs would start glowing with a pattern that mimics a beating heart. Here's a video showing the glowing of the LEDs:

Afterthoughts

Well, it didn't work out too well and I made only two BPMs (sold only one). I think this was probably because electronic projects are not the most romantic gifts in the world. Also, the price was quite high (600 Rs = 10$). But it was a fun project to make and I learnt a few things about selling products and making them marketable.
